Even Strategy & Entry

1: When numbers (2, 4, 6, 8) indicate 11.5% and above, place an Even contract type in all volatilities from Volatility 10 index to Volatility 100 index.

Main Volatility: Volatility 100 index.

Entry Point

Make sure the cursor hits 4 or 5 times on odd numbers, then run your bot if it hits any Even number.

Note: Don't rush! Wait for confirmation of an Even number before running your bot.

Odd Strategy & Entry

3: When numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) indicate 11% and above in all volatilities from Volatility 10 index to Volatility 100 index, place an Odd contract type.

Main Volatility: Volatility 75 index.

Entry Point

Make sure the cursor hits 3 or 4 times on even numbers, then run your bot if it hits any Odd number.

Note: Don't rush! Wait for confirmation of an Odd number before running your bot.
